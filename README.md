## Retail_Customer_behaviour_analysis

## Project Overview

Organizations generate vast amounts of customer transaction data, yet transforming this data into meaningful business insights remains a significant challenge. This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Analytics workflow that converts raw retail customer data into actionable insights to support strategic business decision-making.

The project follows the complete analytics lifecycle—from understanding the business problem and preparing the data to performing SQL-based analysis, developing executive dashboards, and communicating findings through business documentation and presentations.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often struggle to answer critical business questions such as:

- Which customer segments contribute the highest revenue?
- What purchasing patterns exist across different demographics?
- Which product categories drive the majority of sales?
- How do payment preferences and shipping methods influence customer behavior?
- Which customer groups should be targeted to improve business performance?

The objective of this project was to analyze customer shopping behavior and provide data-driven insights that could support marketing, sales, inventory planning, and customer engagement strategies.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior across multiple demographic segments.
- Identify high-performing product categories and seasonal sales trends.
- Understand customer preferences for payment and shipping methods.
- Design an executive dashboard for business stakeholders.
- Generate actionable recommendations using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

## Key Business Insights

Some of the insights generated during the analysis include:

- Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly across demographic segments.
- A small number of product categories contribute a large proportion of customer purchases.
- Payment and shipping preferences exhibit identifiable behavioral patterns.
- Customer purchasing frequency differs across customer groups, creating opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.
- Executive KPI dashboards enable faster monitoring of customer performance and sales trends.
